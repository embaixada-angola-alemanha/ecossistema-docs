## SUS Score Calculation (for facilitator)

```
For odd questions (1, 3, 5, 7, 9):  score = response - 1
For even questions (2, 4, 6, 8, 10): score = 5 - response

SUS Score = (sum of all scores) × 2.5

Interpretation:
  > 80.3  = A (Excellent)
  68-80.3 = B (Good)
  68      = C (Average)
  51-68   = D (Below Average)
  < 51    = F (Poor)
```
