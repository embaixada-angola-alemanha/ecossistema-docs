## 2. Training Plan

### Phase 1: Core Training (Week 1)

| Session | Audience | Duration | Content | Trainer |
|---------|----------|----------|---------|---------|
| T1 | All staff | 1h | System overview, login, navigation | Project Lead |
| T2 | Consular team | 2h | SGC hands-on: cidadão + visa + agendamento | Project Lead |
| T3 | Communications | 2h | SI + WN content management | Project Lead |
| T4 | IT Lead | 3h | Administration, Keycloak, monitoring | Project Lead |

### Phase 2: Advanced Training (Week 2)

| Session | Audience | Duration | Content | Trainer |
|---------|----------|----------|---------|---------|
| T5 | Consular team | 1h | Reports, edge cases, tips & tricks | Project Lead |
| T6 | Communications | 1h | SEO, media optimization, multilingual | Project Lead |
| T7 | IT Lead | 1h | GPJ, deployment, backup procedures | Project Lead |
| T8 | All staff | 30min | Mobile app demo + Q&A | Project Lead |

### Phase 3: Support (Ongoing)

| Activity | Frequency | Format |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| Office hours | First 2 weeks: daily 30min | Video call |
| FAQ updates | As needed | Written document |
| Video library | Ongoing | Recorded tutorials |
| Quick reference | One-time | Printed cards |
